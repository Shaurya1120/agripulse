What AgriPulse Does

We turn crop risk into a clear action plan for enterprises and farmers.

AgriPulse studies a crop and a region, estimates the supply chain disruption risk, explains the real problem behind that risk, and turns it into a practical response plan with loss estimates, charts, and role-specific reporting.

1. Explain

What is happening?

We describe whether the disruption comes from heat wave, crop disease, heavy rainfall, logistics stress, storage problems, or market shocks.

2. Quantify

What is the business impact?

We combine risk with quantity, price, and planning horizon so a buyer or producer can estimate likely rupee exposure.

3. Respond

What should we do next?

We produce role-specific mitigation steps, from alternate sourcing and buffers to farmer resilience actions and scheme awareness.

Who Should Use It

Enterprise Risk Desk

For procurement teams, exporters, processors, and supply chain planners who need disruption narratives, loss estimates, and plan B strategy.

Farmer & Producer Desk

For producers who want to estimate possible loss, understand the cause of disruption, and review practical protection steps with scheme notes.
