About Us

AgriPulse helps buyers, exporters, farmers, and producers understand crop risk fast.

We explain the local disruption, estimate possible loss, and turn the result into a simple action plan with weather, charts, and exportable reports.

What AgriPulse Does

We show what can go wrong with a crop in a place and what to do next.

AgriPulse works for buyers and exporters as well as farmers and producers.

Enter any crop and any location. AgriPulse explains the local problem, estimates possible loss, and gives a simple action plan.

1. Explain

What is happening?

We explain the real issue, like heat wave, crop disease, heavy rain, storage stress, or market shock.

2. Quantify

What is the impact?

We use quantity, rate, and time horizon to estimate likely loss in rupees.

3. Respond

What should we do next?

We give a simple next-step plan for buyers, exporters, farmers, and producers.
